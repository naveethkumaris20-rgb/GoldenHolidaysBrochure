The Old Bangalore
Heritage Experience

Rediscover Bangalore the way it was meant to be experienced

Vehicles Mercedes · Crysta · Urbania
Duration 5–6 Hours
Ideal For NRIs · Parents · Cultural Guests

The Bangalore that made this city extraordinary — its granite temples, its centuries-old bazaars, its layered Dravidian and colonial textures — exists beneath the modern surface, waiting. This is not a city tour. It is a curated heritage immersion into a Bangalore that most residents have never truly seen.

Curated Route

Bull Temple — Nandi in living stone, 16th-century Kempe Gowda legacy
Gavi Gangadhareshwara — A cave temple of astronomical precision
Dodda Ganapathi — One of India's largest Ganesha sculptures, quietly extraordinary
Gandhi Bazaar — A curated heritage walk through Basavanagudi's living history
Traditional premium lunch — a curated finale at a heritage South Indian restaurant

What This Must Feel Like

"Not three temples and lunch. A slow, meaningful journey through the layers of a city that has been accumulating stories for 500 years — guided by someone who knows where they are buried."
